CAFE PROJECT

Team-Based Website Design for a Local Restaurant

Cafe brand identity moodboard

Project Specs:

  • Timeline: Six Months
  • Team: 3 Designers, 3 Developers
  • Outcome: Complete and Finished Website

Skills Used:

  • Wireframing & Prototyping (Figma)
  • User Flows
  • Design Systems & Component Libraries
  • High-Fidelity Mockups

Project Description

I was selected for a team as part of the UW club Web Impact. We were tasked to develop a website for a local business called Cafetal Quilombo Cafe. I worked to develop the mood board and website style alongside my teammates and the restaurant owner. I developed a significant percentage of the low-fidelity mockups for the website, and then we discussed what to do moving forward as a team. I then developed all of the mobile mock-up screens and helped to develop the high-fidelity final screen prototypes for the website.
